Custódio Mata - Lawyer

I graduated from the University of Lisbon, registered at the Portuguese Bar Association since 2013, with registration number 52143e.

Our firm is dedicated to providing services in various areas of law, with a specialisation in Real Estate and Construction Law.
Some common areas of practice include: real estate deeds, tax foreclosures, evictions, negotiating real estate transactions, legal support for emigrants, challenging administrative offences and all day to day problems. See below all the areas in which we operate.

More recently, given the important changes to the urban lease regime recently, we have been advising our clients in all the procedures for updating rents and updating of rents and lease agreements, using the processes that ensure the fastest faster resolution of leases and leasehold restitution.

This office remains at the full disposal of our clients to clarify and follow up any questions that may arise in this area.
We do piecework, consultancy work, or on a retainer basis. Whatever your problem may be, we have a solution for you: you just need to contact us.
